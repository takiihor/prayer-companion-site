# 禱告同行 / Prayer Companion

## 中文（繁體）

**禱告同行**是一個基督徒禱告陪伴應用程式，幫助使用者把痛苦、焦慮、孤單、內疚、軟弱和生命中的困難帶到神面前，並獲得聖經經文與溫柔的鼓勵。

本應用程式提供基於聖經的禱告陪伴與經文鼓勵，但不取代牧師、教會輔導、專業心理治療、醫療服務或緊急危機支援。

## English

**Prayer Companion** is a Christian prayer and Scripture encouragement app that helps users bring pain, anxiety, loneliness, guilt, weakness, and difficult life seasons before God, and receive gentle encouragement grounded in Scripture.

The app provides Bible-based prayer companionship and Scripture encouragement. It does not replace pastors, church counselors, licensed therapists, medical professionals, emergency services, or crisis support.
